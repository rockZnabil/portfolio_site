Fig. 1 (a) Conceptual scheme of a hybrid lab-on-a-chip (LOC) device made of patterned polycarbonate (PC) sheet for urinalysis, (b) urine solution inside a cup, and (c and d) its operational steps. (c) Finger force is applied to initiate negative pressure to move sample solution into the LOC device chamber, and (d) the solution flows into the device chamber to react with the reagent pads.
